Quick tips to spot phishing

  • Check the sender address — is the domain exactly correct?
  • Hover links before clicking to see the real destination URL.
  • Watch for urgency (password reset, immediate action) — attackers use panic to force mistakes.
  • Unexpected attachments or requests for credentials are red flags.
  • When in doubt, report the email to security (or your instructor) instead of clicking.
